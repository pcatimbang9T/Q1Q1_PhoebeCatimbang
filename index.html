Frozen

Frozen is a child-friendly, musical fantasy, animated film made by Walt Disney Animation Studios. This movie highlights the importance of friendship, kindness, trust,and love. It all starts with two sisters--Elsa and Anna. Elsa has super powers which affected Anna, since Elsa accidentally injured her. When their parents passed away, they started being more distant due to Elsa always shutting her out, due to not wanting to hurt Anna. In the end, we learn that sibling love is the most powerful love out there; no matter how much you push them away, they will always love you despite the situations.
